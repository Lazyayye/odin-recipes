Chicken Tacos

Home Page

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Chicken

Directions

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  3. Step 3: Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  4. Step 4: Cook on low for 6-8 hours. Shred chicken with two forks.
